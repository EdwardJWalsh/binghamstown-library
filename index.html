About us

Welcome to Binghamstown library. We are confident that our extensive collection of litertaure will be enough to satiate everones needs from the casual reader who is looking to decrease their screen time to the most avid book worms. Our range of reading materials varies from classic literature to contemperary fiction to graphic novels. However, our library is more than just a building to borrow books from, we pride ourselves in being an intergral part of the community with a wide range of services on offer. Our diverse team of experinced librarians are always happy to help with any queries you may have and our well stocked complimentary tea and coffee station is ever popular for anyone who would like a jolt of caffiene while browsing our shelves.
