Services

As well as our extensive range of titles and genres we provide many more services for the community


Read with me


Here at Binghamstown library we believe in nurturing a life long love of reading from an early age. We have carefully curated an extensive collection of childrens books which we hope will spark a love of story telling and foster a deep appreciation for the love of reading. This is why we offer our 'Interactive Storytime' sessions. Let our animated story tellers bring all of your childrens favourite characters to life in a way which is both immersive and entertaining. Children are encouraged to suggest their favourite books for our storytellers to bring to life. Read-with-me sessions take place every Saturday at 12pm.


Computer classes for over 60s


New technology can be daunting no matter what age you are but if you are from the generation that didn't grow up with the internet or with a device constantly in your hand it can be especially daunting. Join one of our computer classes and let our IT specialist Derek guide you through the information super highway in a way that is catered to anyone with any level of experience. We are confident that in no time at all you will be able to keep up with the younger generation. Topics covered include 'How to use Google', 'Sending e-mails' and 'Setting up a social media profile' among many many others. Join us on Thursday evenings at 6pm. Register your interest at the bottom of this page or in-person at any time by speaking to one of our librarians.


Computers for public use


There are a number of computers available that are free to use for members of the library. We also offer printing and scanning services. As with all of our services we have dedicated members of our team to assist you with anything you may need.


Rent your favourite movie


Can't find your favourite movie on your streaming service? We have an extensive collection of DVDs and Blu-rays on offer from classic black and white movies of old Hollywood to current indie movies and everything in between.


Board game nights


Reading not really your thing? Join us for our board game nights every Tuesday from 6pm until 8pm. Whether you are a chess master or just a casual scrabble player our wide range of board games for all ages provide a much needed social outlet for the community.


Genealogy service


Interested in tracing your roots? Our dedicated local historian and genealogy specialist will be on site every Wednesday afternoon from 2pm. Book an appointment by emailing us or by speaking to a member of staff at any time.
